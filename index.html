Smart Energy Management System

Leveraging IoT and Data Analytics for a Sustainable Future

Abstract

In modern organizations, significant energy is wasted when electrical appliances are left running in unoccupied spaces. Our research presents an integrated Smart Energy Management System that monitors device-level consumption using IoT sensors (ACS712, PIR, RCWL) and provides AI-driven optimization strategies. The system enables real-time monitoring and automated recommendations to reduce operational costs and carbon footprints.
